常用运行命令：

bash
停止保存巡检服务：
systemctl disable road_patrol.service
systemctl stop road_patrol.service

启动实时显示：
systemctl start road_realtime.service

停止实时显示：
systemctl stop road_realtime.service

重启实时显示：
systemctl restart road_realtime.service

查看实时显示日志：
journalctl -u road_realtime.service -f

确认是否开机自启：
systemctl is-enabled road_realtime.service

手动运行实时程序：
cd /userdata/rknn_yolov8_demo
export LD_LIBRARY_PATH=$PWD/lib:$LD_LIBRARY_PATH
./rknn_yolov8_realtime model/yolov8.rknn
